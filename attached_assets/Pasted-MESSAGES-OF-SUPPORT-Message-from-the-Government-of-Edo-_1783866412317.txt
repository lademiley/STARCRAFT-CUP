MESSAGES OF SUPPORT 
 
Message from the Government of Edo State 
The Government of Edo State warmly welcomes the StarCraft Cup and 
commends the vision behind this important grassroots football initiative. 
The tournament represents a valuable platform for youth development, 
talent discovery, and community engagement, all of which align with the 
state's commitment to empowering young people through sports. 
Edo State has always invested in sports development, infrastructure, and 
youth programs, recognizing the role of sports in promoting discipline, 
unity, and economic growth. The StarCraft Cup provides an opportunity 
to further strengthen these efforts by creating pathways for talented 
young footballers to showcase their abilities and pursue their dreams. 
The government encourages participating teams, coaches, officials, and 
supporters to embrace the values of fair play, excellence, and 
sportsmanship throughout the competition. We believe that this 
tournament will contribute positively to the development of football and 
inspire a new generation of athletes across Edo State and beyond. 
The Government of Edo State wishes the organizers every success and 
looks forward to the lasting impact the StarCraft Cup will have on our 
communities and our youth.

Message from His Royal Majesty, the Oba of Benin 
His Royal Majesty, the Oba of Benin, extends royal blessings and 
goodwill to the organizers, participants, and supporters of the StarCraft 
Cup. 
Sports, particularly football, have long served as a unifying force that 
brings together people from different communities and backgrounds. 
The promotion of youth development through organized sporting 
activities reflects values that are deeply respected within the Benin 
Kingdom, including discipline, hard work, respect, and excellence. 
The Oba of Benin commends the efforts of the organizers in creating a 
platform that encourages young people to develop their talents while 
fostering friendship, unity, and healthy competition. Such initiatives 
contribute to the growth of society and provide hope and opportunities 
for future generations. 
His Royal Majesty encourages all participants to compete honorably, 
respect one another, and serve as worthy ambassadors of their 
communities. The StarCraft Cup is not only a football competition but 
also a celebration of youth, culture, and the enduring spirit of the people. 
The Palace of the Oba of Benin offers its support and best wishes for a 
successful tournament and prays for peace, safety, and excellence 
throughout the competition.
